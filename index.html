One thing I am thankful for is:

The Internet

The internet has changed everyone's lives. For me, it has made my life so much better. All of my main interests and hobbies involve the internet, so I don't know what I would do in my free time without it. The internet also allows me to talk to people anytime anywhere. This means I am able to still talk to my sister who is in college, or my grandparents who live several miles away while I'm in my own house. Most importantly though, I have made several lifelong friends through the internet, who I likely would have never met or become friends with otherwise.
